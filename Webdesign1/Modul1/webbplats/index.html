Champagnebutiken

I also work as an importer of wine and Champagne från France and Italy, I sell to bars and restaurants, but also directly to private consumers. Due to the laws regulating the sells of alcohol in Sweden, I started a company in Denmark to be able to sell directly to the consumers through a procedure called distance-selling. This means that the goods is sent directly from a warehouse in Denmark to the consumers home adress. Alcohol tax and VAT is payed in Sweden. These are the different types of wine you can buy from our Danish webshop:

  1. White wine from Italy
  2. Red wine from Italy
  3. Champagne from the French Champagne region
  4. Ratafia from the French Champagne region
  5. Merchandise like stoppers, aprons, corkscrews, etc
